The meaning of "apples and pears"

" Apples and pears "
Meaning:
Cockney rhyming slang for stairs.
Example:
Time for bed Jimmy - get yourself up the apples and pears.
Where did it originate?:
Britain.
Where is it used?:
Mostly Britain.
